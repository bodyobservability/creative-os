# Accounting Operator (v1.1)

## Purpose
The Accounting Operator is a first-class Creative-OS domain responsible for **evidence capture, provenance, classification, and export** of financial artifacts.

## Interface
**Inputs**
- EmailMessage (.eml)
- Attachment (binary)
- ManualReceipt (PDF/Image)
- PortalInvoice (PDF/HTML)

**Outputs**
- ReceiptBundle
- LedgerDrafts
- TaxExports

## Invariants
- Originals are immutable (write-once).
- All transforms are append-only.
- Every output references evidence via hashes.
- Time-bounded epochs are never rewritten.

## Non-Goals
- No bookkeeping or double-entry.
- No payments or reconciliation.
- No inbox mutation.
